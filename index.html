Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about growing restaurant sales through DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, Toast, and direct online ordering.

What exactly does Blender Digital do?

We manage and grow your restaurant’s online ordering channels. That can include DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, Toast, direct ordering, promotional campaigns, Sponsored Listings, menu and storefront optimization, review strategy, reporting, and ongoing performance improvements.

Which restaurant delivery platforms do you manage?

We primarily manage DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, Toast, direct online ordering systems, and catering platforms such as ezCater when applicable. The exact strategy depends on where your restaurant currently sells and where the strongest growth opportunities exist.

How quickly can we expect to see results?

Many restaurants begin seeing measurable changes within 30 to 60 days, especially when there are clear opportunities involving promotions, menu organization, Sponsored Listings, or storefront quality. More substantial growth usually requires consistent optimization over several months.

Will we need to hire another employee?

No. Blender Digital acts as an extension of your team. We handle strategy, platform management, campaign planning, performance reviews, and ongoing recommendations so you do not need a full-time employee dedicated to managing the delivery apps.

Do you guarantee results?

No responsible agency should guarantee a specific sales number. We commit to active management, clear recommendations, ongoing testing, and decisions based on actual performance data—not guesswork.

Who is Blender Digital a good fit for?

We are a strong fit for restaurant owners and operators who already sell through online ordering platforms, want more consistent management, care about both sales and profitability, and are willing to test and improve their offers.
