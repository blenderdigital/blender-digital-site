## Positioning rationale (from competitor research, July 2026)

- **Owner.com** (category's biggest Meta spender, ~95 active ads, ~15 new/week) leads with an *anti-third-party* angle: "escape 30% DoorDash fees, own your customers," proven with dashboard screen recordings, split screens, and owner testimonials with specific dollar figures.
- **Spice Digital** targets multi-location operators: "grow off-premise revenue, no extra headcount."
- **Pimentón** positions as a "growth partner" with a dedicated growth manager and data team.
- **DoorDash/Uber Eats merchant ads** are stat-led ("$6 earned per $1 spent on ads", "1.7x sales with promo + ad") in flat brand-color layouts.

**Blender Digital's wedge:** everyone else either tells owners to *leave* the apps (Owner.com) or sells them self-serve tools (platforms). Blender is the only one saying *your third-party channels are an asset that just needs professional management* — so the creatives lean into "win ON the apps," proven with a real client dashboard result, priced transparently.
